What should I get paid?



Instructions

1. Input your previous yearly salary in the first box. You can find this number in you November payslip and multiplying by 13. It's specifically the line that says Four Weekly Salary.

2. Input your new yearly salary in the second box. You can find this on fourth or you can ask your line manager as this will have been recently communicated to you. Type this in as a number with no commas.

Note: Make sure you only type in numbers and remove commas if you have copied and pasted

3. Select Submit and your December wage should be displayed


* Please note this does not factor in other benefits or bonuses you may expect to see.
